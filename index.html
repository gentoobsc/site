How it Works

Gentoo is a community driven token with a selling tax, holder re-distribution, auto-liquidity addition and a lottery.

Why is Gentoo better?

We are not a simple fork like the rest! The Gentoo team have added in a host of differentiating functionality into our smart contract,including:

  • An un-vested lottery dapp that burns tokens as holders play

  • Max-buy and anti-whale technology, including blacklists

Best Features

We have a list of features that seperate us from the rest!

Lottery Dapp

Only Gentoo holders have acccess to spin to win the lottery jackpot. Simply cash your tokens into a ticket and spin to win it all! Each spin is a 1 in 1000 chance to scoop the pot!

Deflationary

Every time someone plays the lottery, the ticket (Gentoo tokens) used to play is sent directly to the dead address - effectively burned forever!

Redistribution

On every sell there is a 4% redistribution fee that is spread amongst all holders. This also occurs while you are vested, simply hold and earn.

Rising Jackpot

The lottery jackpot is increased as 1% of every sell is added directly to the Lottery Contract. Gentoo sent to play the lottery is burned, increasing demand and lowering supply.

Frequently Asked questions

Find quick and easy answers to the most common questions we're consistently asked

How can I participate in the lottery?

After clicking the lottery link in the menu, connect your wallet. You must hold enough Gentoo to afford a ticket. Choose a number between 0 and 999 on the slider and click the play button. Your choice will be sent off to the smart contract and a number between 0 and 999 will be randomized. Should your number match the contract's - the entire BSC amount held by the smart contract will be transferred to your address. The Gentoo you sent will be burned (sent to the dead address) forever.

Where can I buy and sell Gentoo?

Gentoo is currently tradeable on PANCAKESWAP, which is a decentralized exchange on the BSC network. Visit the Gentoo dashboard above and click on the buy button to make a purchase.
